Description

The adidas Yeezy Boost 350 V2 “Earth” is a North America-exclusive colorway of Kanye West’s highly sought-after silhouette. Released in February 2020, the regional design features a brown Primeknit upper that resembles dirt, hence the "Earth" nickname. The Yeezy 350's traditional stripe appears with a translucent thread in the same brown hue. Adidas opts to equip the shoe with light brown laces and uses the shade again on the back heel tab. A tonal midsole and slightly darker outsole completes the "Earth" Yeezys.
